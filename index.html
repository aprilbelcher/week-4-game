The Crystal Collector

Instruction

There are four crystals displayed as buttons on the page. You will see a random number at the start of the game. When you click on a crystal, it will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You lose if their score goes above the random number.You win if your total score matches the random number from the beginning of the game.
